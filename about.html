ABOUT US

OUR HISTORY

we are a family and the heartbeat of our llocal community

Founded in 2010, Sierra Leone Football Agency began with a simple mission: to discover and nurture football talent across Sierra Leone. Starting from a small office in Freetown, we have grown to become one of West Africa's most respected football agencies.

Over the years, we have successfully placed over 50 players in professional clubs across Africa, Europe, and Asia, while maintaining strong relationships with local Sierra Leone Premier League clubs.

OUR VISION

At our football agency we envision a future where raw talent meets the right opportuity we are committed to discovering developing and representing players

To become the leading football agency in West Africa, recognized globally for developing world-class talent from Sierra Leone and creating pathways for African footballers to succeed at the highest levels of professional football.

We envision a future where Sierra Leone becomes a powerhouse of football talent in Africa, with our agency playing a pivotal role in this transformation.

OUR MISSION

we are committed to discovering developing and representing players

Our mission is to identify, develop, and promote exceptional football talent from Sierra Leone, providing comprehensive support throughout their careers. We are committed to:

  • Scouting and discovering raw talent across Sierra Leone
  • Providing professional training and development programs
  • Securing opportunities with professional clubs worldwide
  • Offering career guidance and contract management
  • Supporting players' personal and professional growth
  • Promoting Sierra Leone football on the global stage
